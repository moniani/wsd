About Us

Alive Design was founded by Daniel Smith in 2010. Daniel received his Bachelor's in Architecture from the University of Illinois in Chicago. Soon after, he decided to combine his knowledge of urban design with his lifelong passion for horticulture in this landscaping co. His work changes plain lawns into beautiful and peaceful environments.
